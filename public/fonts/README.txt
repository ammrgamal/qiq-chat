Place Arabic font files here for pdfmake dynamic loading:
- NotoNaskhArabic-Regular.ttf
- NotoKufiArabic-Regular.ttf

You can download from:
https://fonts.google.com/noto/specimen/Noto+Naskh+Arabic
https://fonts.google.com/noto/specimen/Noto+Kufi+Arabic

These are used by public/js/pdf-arabic-fonts.js to enable proper Arabic shaping in generated PDFs.
